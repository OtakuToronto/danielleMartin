Dragonsmith

(2017-2018)

Dragonsmith is an exploration of the anatomy behind the greatest of mythical beasts, the dragon. Dragons have existed in some form or another across human folklore, myth and fiction for centuries. But what exactly makes a dragon a dragon?

This edu-tainment project is all about exploring something legendary in a new light. Dragonsmith is set in a fictional world in which dragons are not born, but instead made. Consumers take on the role of Dragonsmiths, building on the creatures and folklore of the past to create the dragons of the future. Guided by Master Dragonsmiths Lump and Yong, they can learn about the many dragon “parts” in their arsenal using the Dragonsmith Guide to Dragon Anatomy. Testing their skills as a new Dragonsmith, consumers use cards representing anatomy “parts” to build dragons of their own. Accompanying this game is an expanding array of Dragonsmith merchandise including children's book The Great Dragon Collaboration, enamel pins and plush toys.
